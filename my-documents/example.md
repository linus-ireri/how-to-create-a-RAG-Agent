Lino.AI Company Overview and FAQ

About Lino.AI

Lino.AI is a technology company focused on building ethical, accessible, and modular AI tools for developers and businesses. Founded in Nairobi, Kenya, the company believes that powerful AI should be available to builders everywhere, not just in large tech hubs.

Our mission is to simplify the process of building AI-powered applications, particularly those that need to work with custom knowledge bases and domain-specific information.

Products and Services

Lino.AI offers several core products:

The RAG Toolkit is our flagship open-source library for building Retrieval-Augmented Generation pipelines in Node.js. It provides simple, well-documented APIs for document ingestion, vector storage, and LLM integration.

The Chatbot Studio is a no-code interface for businesses to build and deploy custom chatbots trained on their own documents and knowledge bases. No programming experience required.

The Embeddings API provides fast, low-cost text embedding as a service, powered by open-source models hosted on Lino.AI infrastructure.

Pricing

Lino.AI operates on a freemium model. The open-source RAG Toolkit is completely free. The Chatbot Studio offers a free tier supporting up to 5 documents and 100 queries per month. Paid plans start at $19 per month for unlimited documents and 5,000 queries.

The Embeddings API offers 1 million tokens free per month, with additional usage billed at $0.0001 per 1,000 tokens.

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days and returned to the original payment method.

Technical Requirements

The RAG Toolkit requires Node.js version 18 or higher. It runs on Windows, macOS, and Linux. The embedding models run locally on your machine using the CPU — no GPU required, though a GPU will significantly speed up ingestion for large document collections.

Minimum recommended system specifications are 4GB of RAM and 2GB of free disk space for the model cache.

Support and Contact

Support is available via email at support@lino.ai and through our community Discord server. Response times for email support are typically within 24 hours on business days.

For enterprise customers, dedicated support with a 4-hour response SLA is available on the Enterprise plan.

Our documentation is available at docs.lino.ai and covers everything from quick-start guides to advanced RAG tuning techniques.

Privacy and Data

Lino.AI takes data privacy seriously. Documents you upload to the Chatbot Studio are stored encrypted and are never used to train our models or shared with third parties. You can delete your data at any time from the account settings page.

For self-hosted deployments using the open-source RAG Toolkit, all data remains entirely on your own infrastructure.
